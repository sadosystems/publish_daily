---
title: "Additive color as creative metaphor"
---

The way this site blends red, green, and blue for painting, coding, and writing feels accidentally profound. When you do all three, the result isn't muddy. It's bright. Almost white. I like the idea that creative pursuits combine additively. Writing doesn't take away from painting. Coding doesn't diminish writing. They illuminate each other.
