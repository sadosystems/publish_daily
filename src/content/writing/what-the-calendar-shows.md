---
title: "What the calendar shows"
---

Looking at a year of creative work compressed into colored squares is a strange experience. Some weeks are dense with activity, every cell lit up. Others are sparse. The temptation is to judge the sparse weeks as failures, but when I click into them I often find that those were the weeks I was thinking. Germinating.
