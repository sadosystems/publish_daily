---
title: "Against optimization"
---

Everyone wants to optimize their creative process. Wake up at 5am. Use the Pomodoro technique. Batch your tasks. Eliminate distractions. I've tried all of it and the thing that actually works is much simpler and much harder: care about what you're making. When I care, the hours disappear.
